DoThis — offline / local copy
=============================

You do not need the internet to use these guides once this folder is on your computer.

How to open
-----------
1. Open this folder.
2. Double-click index.html
3. Your browser will show the DoThis home page.
4. Search or click a guide. Print if you want a paper copy.

Larger text
-----------
Use the "AA" button in the top-right. Your choice stays on this computer.

Sharing with family
-------------------
Copy this whole folder to a USB stick or send the zip. They open index.html the same way.

Privacy
-------
Nothing you type in the search box is uploaded by DoThis.
There is no account and no payment.
