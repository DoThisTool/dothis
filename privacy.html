DoThis privacy policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

In one sentence

DoThis is a free set of how‑to guides that run in your browser. We do not require an account, and we do not use advertising trackers to follow what you read.

What DoThis is

DoThis helps people complete everyday digital tasks — like saving a photo, sharing Wi‑Fi, or printing a page — with short, plain‑English steps. It is a static website (and can be saved as files on your computer for offline use).

Data we do not collect

  • We do not require an email, login, or account.
  • We do not use advertising pixels, social trackers, or fingerprinting scripts.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not need payment information — the guides are free.

What stays on your device

The “larger text” button may save a simple preference in your browser’s localStorage so the choice is remembered next time. That setting never leaves your device through DoThis.

Search and category filters run entirely in your browser using the guide data already loaded with the page. Your search words are not sent to a DoThis server for analysis (there is no search backend).

If you use a hosted copy of the site

Like any website, the computer or service that hosts the static files may keep standard technical server logs (for example IP address, browser type, and which pages were requested). Those logs are for operating and securing the site — not for building advertising profiles of which guides you prefer.

If DoThis is later published on a free host (for example GitHub Pages), that host’s own logging practices apply to the request for the files themselves.

Offline / local copy

You can download or copy the DoThis folder and open index.html from your computer. Offline use does not phone home for guide content. You stay in control of the files.

Links to other sites

Guides may mention apps or websites (for example YouTube or Windows Settings). Those products have their own privacy policies. DoThis does not control them.

Children

DoThis does not target children with advertising or accounts. Guides are general‑audience how‑tos. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.

Contact

DoThis is a small public‑good project (separate from PolicySnap and CalmClick, which share similar values). Prefer the project repository contact if one is published alongside the site: github.com/PolicySnapAdmin/dothis.
