    Why DoThis exists

    Technology should not require a relative on speed dial. These guides exist to make ordinary digital life a little easier, simpler, and more honest.

    • No shame If nobody showed you how, that isn’t your fault. Zero jargon assumed.
    • No catch Free to use. No account. No “start free trial.” We don’t need your email.
    • No data grab Guides run in your browser. We don’t build ad profiles from what you open.
    • You’re done when… Every guide says what success looks like, so you can stop fretting.
